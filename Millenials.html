Millenials (1981 - 1996)

The Benefits For Millenials

The Implimentation Of Digital Media Into The Lives Of Millenials

Millenials are the first generation to grow up with the digital age. At the beginning of this generation, there wasn't a widespread use of the internet quite yet, with only 1% of the millenial population using the internet in 1988. However, by 1993, approximately 50% of millenials were using the internet.

Benefits of Digital Media

  • Access to Information
    • Widespread access to an enormous amount of information was for the first time accessible to any generation during the millienial's generation.
    • This access to information allowed for new learning styles and skills to be learned very quickly.
  • Much Easier Worldwide Communication
    • Due to the invention of the internet, communication became much easier to do.
    • Meeting new people became less of a problem as well.
    • Sharing information for any subject became much easier which allowed a lot more progress in every field.
  • Work Benefits
    • New jobs were invented due to the new digital age, such as:
      • Social Media Manager
      • Data Analyst
    • Increased productivity by allowing people to access documents from anywhere they are as well as being able to contact anyone anywhere.

Negatives of Digital Media

  • Too reliant on technology/social media
    • 74% of millenials say that technology makes their lives easier, which can indicate a reliance on it
  • Impacted interpersonal skills
    • Due to constantly being behind technological devices and using them for interpersonal connections, millenials may have been impacted in a few ways.
      • Difficulty making eye contact
      • Trouble connecting with people in person
  • Negative cognitive performance
    • Increase of multitasking due to technology has a negative affect on cognitive performance
    • Reduced attention spans
    • The desire for instant gratification results in impatience
