What Influence Does Today's Digital Media Have On Different Generations?

The Affects On Generations

Today's digital media has both many positive and negative affects on every generation, however, some generations are affected much differently than others.

Credit: researchgate.net

Positive

  • Overall global connection
  • Increased productivity in every field of life
  • Different ways of learning information
  • Increased creativity
  • Economical benefits found in every aspect of the field

Negative

  • Decreased attention spans
  • Overall less vocabulary usage due to less books being read
  • Traditional learning methods are becoming ineffective
  • Cognitive overload
  • Increased amount of mental issues across all generations

Overall, the mass increase of digital media as a whole is definitley a net positive for the good of humanity, but it is necessary to take a look at the negatives of something good as well.
