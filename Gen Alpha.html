Gen Alpha (2013 - 2028)

Gen "The Digital Natives" Alpha

They are the first generation to be born entirely in the 21st century, and they are often referred to as the “next generation” or “digital natives.” Generation Alpha is shaped by technology and is considered to be more connected, more globally aware, and more open-minded than previous generations. They are also the most racially and ethnically diverse generation in history.

What Has Changed For Gen Alpha?

  • Online relationships
    • Gen Alpha has a lot of online friendships with people they have never met. (nearly 25%)
    • Physical relationships have become a lot harder to start and maintain with this generation, due to the amount of time spent online and not practicing those interpersonal skills.
  • Learning styles
    • Due to the low attention span, visual learning and communication through digital images is at the center of their development.
    • Learn vast amounts of new skills that deals with technology, such as learning styles and different ways of socializing.
  • Creativity and imagination
    • Students focus more on online games rather than physical toys, which allows for more creative ideas and thinking in today's online era
  • Physical and mental health
    • Physical and mental health have both been severely impacted due to the increased amount of time spent inside
    • Decreased attention spans have been another effect

What's Next For This Generation?

Despite all of the negative ailments that have been seen within this generation to do this new age run off of technology and digital media, there is a lot of good that has yet to be seen that will come from it. There is no real way to know how this will affect the workforce for whenever Gen Alpha becomes of working age.
